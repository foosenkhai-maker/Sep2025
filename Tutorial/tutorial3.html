4 Study habits that you should develop

1.Find a good place to study

Finding a good location to study is one of the most important step to take before you start to study because if you don't find a good location to study you might get distracted by something and it will cause a huge affection when you are studying something and it would be hard for you to focus, so make sure to find a good location for your study session. You can find some of the location like library or your own room to study as long as it does not have any distraction when you study.

2.take a break

Studying too long might affect your brain memory because your brain can't consume everything and if you keep forcing yourself to continue studying everything after you have been studying for a long hour, it would be hard for you to memorize everything because your brain is tired. That is why you can't study for so long and you need to take a break to let your brain rest in order to study more things, so make sure to give yourself a break at least half or one hour.

3.Study with a group

Study alone is not wrong but it would be better if you have a group to study together because it will make you understand more and fast, some of the people in the group might understand what you don't understand, so asking them would be easier than you go find those informations by yourself and it saves you more time.

4.Reward yourself

There's a lot of way to make study more interesting and one of the way is reward yourself after you finished your study. It can make you feel joy and also satisfied and the reward that you give to yourself after you finished your study would be meaningful for you just like you just finished an important mission and you got something special. you could reward yourself by buying a food, drink, or an item for yourself. This way, you won't feel empty after you finished your study.

Developing good study habits can lead to better academic performance, lower stress, and a more balanced life.
