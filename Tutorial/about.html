About us

Our approach blends foundational computer science with practical application and personal development.

  1. Philosophy

  • Learn by making

    • Students build games, apps, websites, and small hardware projects to internalize concepts.

  • Skills for life

    • We teach problem solving, collaboration, and ethical online behaviour alongside technical topics.

  • Inclusive and progressive

    • Lessons scale from basic digital literacy to intermediate programming and computing concepts so every student progresses at the right pace.

  1. Team

  • Experienced educators who design age-appropriate lessons and mentor project work.

  • Supportive community of parents and teachers who celebrate learning milestones.

  1. Outcomes

  • Foundations in algorithms, logic, and computational thinking.

  • Practical abilities: coding, web design, simple electronics, and troubleshooting.

  • Confidence to explore advanced topics and pursue computing in senior school.
