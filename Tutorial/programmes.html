Our programmes

Clear, modular programmes designed by age and skill level with measurable learning goals.

  1. Primary Years Programme

    • Focus:

      • Digital literacy, block-based coding, safe internet habits, creative digital projects.

    • Examples:

      • Interactive stories, simple animations, and logic puzzles.

  2. Lower Secondary Programme

    • Focus:

      • Text-based programming introduction, web fundamentals, digital design, and basic hardware.

    • Examples:

      • HTML/CSS mini-sites, Python fundamentals, microcontroller lamp projects.

  3. Upper Secondary Programme

    • Focus:

      • Deeper programming, data basics, systems thinking, cybersecurity awareness, and project portfolios.

    • Examples:

      • Mobile app prototypes, simple databases, network basics, capstone projects.

    Enrichment Workshops

    - Short courses in robotics, game design, cloud basics, and creative coding for after school or holiday sessions.

    - Teacher training sessions to help classroom teachers integrate computing into other subjects.
